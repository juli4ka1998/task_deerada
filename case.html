The task

We were faced with the task of developing a sophisticated, multi-functional web platform that boasts a sea of possibilities for searching perspective players, watching hockey match videos, and features footage of individual players with a detailed breakdown of their playing style. Such a large project necessitated breaking up development into several stages and every step of the journey was accomplished by team.

What we did

This is a trailblazing scouting system which supports multiple roles: clubs, players, scouts, agents, and trainers. Each identity involves its own distinctive interface. The system’s development was guided by the double goals of centralizing and formulating a player database and hockey matches for the purposes of increasing international hockey qualifications.

Technologies

We created simple, consistent page templates, making it easier for the team to perform updates to the website. From adding new items to editing blocks of content, we developed a cohesive design system with an intuitive content management experience using WooCommerce WordPress plugin.

In addition to interesting and useful animal care tips the site contains a convenient online store where you can buy different goods for all kinds of pets.

Call to action

Every site should have a purpose and effectively sell a service or product.

A bright color palette

With the purpose of creating an impactful and ownable identity online, we developed a fresh and bold color palette to complement the open, light background of the website.

Inviting typography

We selected a sophisticated yet friendly type treatment for use throughout the website. Easy-to-read with a touch of personality, various weights help communicate brand essence.

Designed with mobile in mind

With an emphasis on mobile-first users, the new website is built to work seamlessly across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
